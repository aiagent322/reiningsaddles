About This Saddle

This Equine Oasis reiner by Tim Bauer is one of the larger-seat options in David Solum's current inventory, making it an ideal choice for the taller or broader-framed competitor who wants a quality competition saddle without sacrificing fit. The 17" seat provides extra room while maintaining the low rise and balanced position critical to reining performance.

The star pattern tooling is consistent throughout the entire saddle — skirts, fenders, back jockey, and swells — in rich dark chocolate leather. The Equine Oasis brand stamp is clearly visible on the owner's side, and sterling silver conchos complete the look. Rear dee rings are included for breast collar attachment.

At $2,995 this is solid competition-quality value in a seat size that's genuinely hard to find in the used market. Certified by David Solum.
