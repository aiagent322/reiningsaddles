About This Saddle

This Kyle Tack Reiner is in exceptional mint / like-new condition — one of the cleanest pre-owned saddles currently in David Solum's inventory. Kyle Tack saddles are handcrafted in Whitesboro, Texas, and have earned a strong following in the reining community for their quality construction, beautiful star pattern tooling, and competitive price point relative to the workmanship.

The 16" black smooth leather seat is the most popular size and finish combination in NRHA competition. Sterling silver conchos and engraved rear dee rings complete the package. The leather throughout shows zero significant wear — this saddle has clearly been well cared for and sparingly used.

Shipping is available — contact David Solum directly for a shipping quote to your location. Certified by David Solum at $3,995 plus shipping.
