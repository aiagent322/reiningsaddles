About This Saddle

A genuinely eye-catching piece — a roughout reiner with striking two-tone black and tan tooling and white buck-stitch trim throughout. The roughout seat and fenders provide exceptional grip, making this an excellent competition choice for Non Pro riders who want security in a sliding stop. The black-and-tan contrast is distinctive in the show pen. Contact David Solum for maker information, seat size, and pricing.
