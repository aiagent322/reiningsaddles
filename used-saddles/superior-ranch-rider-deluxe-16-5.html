The Ranch Rider Deluxe from AM Superior Saddlery is built for the rider who wants versatility without sacrificing craftsmanship. This example features Superior's #1 flower hand tooling — a full-coverage floral pattern that covers every panel from swell to skirt. The deep mahogany/oxblood leather is rich and even, and the elephant-print seat insert provides excellent grip and durability.

Scalloped round silver conchos accent the front and rear dees. The AM Superior Saddlery hang tag is still attached. At 16½ inches, this is one of the larger seat sizes in the inventory and an ideal fit for a taller or broader-framed rider. Contact David to discuss tree width for your horse.
