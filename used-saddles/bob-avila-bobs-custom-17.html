This Bob Avila signature model from Bob's Custom Saddles stands out from other Bob Avila listings with its distinctive S-scroll and wave border tooling — a more refined, understated pattern compared to the heavy basketweave or floral builds. The mahogany leather is rich and even, and the smooth leather seat shows excellent condition with clean stitching throughout.

Sterling silver oval conchos with pendant keepers accent the front and rear dees. The "BOB AVILA" stamp is clearly visible on the fender. At 17 inches, this is an excellent fit for riders who need a larger seat in a dedicated reining build. Condition is rated great — a strong value at this price point.
