The Standard

What "David Solum Certified" Means

🌳

Tree Check

Tree integrity verified — no twists, breaks, or structural issues. Flex test performed.

🔩

Rigging

All rigging hardware checked for cracks, wear, and proper function.

🛠️

Leather

All leather surfaces assessed and documented. Cracking and repairs noted.

📏

Measurements

Seat size, gullet width, and tree width measured and verified accurate.

📋

Full Report

Complete condition report provided with every certified saddle.
