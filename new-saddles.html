The Foundation

SYMMETREES™ — Built Different

The Most Precise, Symmetrical Saddle Tree You'll Find

The very base of every Superior saddle — the saddle tree — is made in-house and is the result of A. Maschke's decade-long research, testing, designing, and constant improvement of new saddle tree concepts. SYMMETREES™ are precise, consistent, and reproducible: even after many years, Superior can deliver a replacement tree with the same fit and profile as the original.

The breeding goals of disciplines such as reining, cutting, and western pleasure have become increasingly specialized over the last 20 years. In close cooperation with some of the most successful trainers in the world, Maschke developed a variety of saddle trees that not only cover today's performance horses, but have been shown to improve performance of horse and rider in show rings worldwide.

Superior has been manufacturing its own saddle trees since 2003, placing special emphasis on symmetry and contemporary fit. Highest customer satisfaction is guaranteed with:
