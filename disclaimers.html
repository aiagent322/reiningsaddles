Site Information

Commercial Disclaimers

These disclosures are intended to make the buyer experience clear and professional. Buyers should confirm the exact saddle, price, condition, shipping terms, and fit details directly with David Solum before making a purchase decision.

Inventory and availability

Used saddle inventory changes. A saddle shown on ReiningSaddles.com may be available, pending, sold, or awaiting updated status. All buyers should call, email, or WhatsApp David Solum to confirm current availability before relying on any listing.

Condition and descriptions

Used saddle descriptions are based on available information, visible features, maker identification, measurements, photos, and seller-provided details. Normal wear, prior repairs, tree condition, hardware condition, and exact measurements should be confirmed before purchase.

Saddle fit

No website can guarantee saddle fit for a specific horse or rider from photos and written specifications alone. Seat size, tree width, skirt length, rigging, rider build, horse back shape, discipline, and intended use all matter. Buyers are encouraged to ask detailed fit questions before purchase.

Pricing, shipping, taxes, customs, and VAT

Prices, freight, insurance, taxes, customs duties, VAT, brokerage charges, and import rules may change and may vary by buyer location. Domestic and international buyers should confirm total cost and responsibility for all charges before ordering.

Sponsors and commercial relationships

ReiningSaddles.com is an educational and commercial saddle resource. Superior Saddlery and Certified Used Saddles may be sponsors, inventory sources, or commercial partners. Sponsorship does not guarantee that any particular saddle is appropriate for a specific horse, rider, competition, or budget.

Final purchase decision

The buyer is responsible for confirming fit, condition, availability, shipping arrangements, import obligations, return terms, and payment details before completing a transaction.
